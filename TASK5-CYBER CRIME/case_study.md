# Cyber Crime Case Study

## What Happened
One day, a person got a call from someone who said they were calling from the bank. The caller sounded convincing and said there was an issue with the account and it needed to be verified.

The caller asked for OTP and some bank details. The person thought it was genuine and shared the OTP.

After a few minutes, money was deducted from the bank account without permission.

---

## Type of Cyber Crime
This is a case of online fraud and phishing.

---

## What Was Done After
As soon as the person realized something was wrong, they called the cyber crime helpline 1930. They also reported the incident on the cyber crime portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in).

---

## Result
Because the complaint was made quickly, the authorities started the process. In some cases, quick action can help in stopping or recovering the money.

---

## What We Learn
- Never share OTP with anyone  
- Banks never ask for such details on call  
- Always double check before trusting unknown calls  
- Report the issue immediately if something goes wrong
