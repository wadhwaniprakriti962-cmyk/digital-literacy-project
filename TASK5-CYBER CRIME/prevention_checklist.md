# Cyber Crime Prevention Checklist

## Things we should do
- Use strong passwords and don’t keep them too simple  
- Turn on two-factor authentication if possible  
- Check links before clicking on them  
- Use secure websites (https)  
- Keep phone and apps updated  
- Report any suspicious activity quickly  

---

## Things to avoid
- Don’t share OTP or passwords with anyone  
- Don’t click on unknown or random links  
- Don’t trust calls from unknown numbers easily  
- Avoid doing transactions on public Wi-Fi  
- Don’t share personal details on random websites  

---

## Important Note
If any cyber fraud happens:
Call 1930 immediately and report on https://cybercrime.gov.in

Quick action can really help.
