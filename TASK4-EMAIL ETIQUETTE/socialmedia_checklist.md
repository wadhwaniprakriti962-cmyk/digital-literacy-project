#Social Media Etiquette Checklist
A quick reference guide for maintaining a safe, professional, and positive presence on social media platforms.

##The Do's
* Use respectful and polite language: Always communicate with courtesy, even in disagreements.
* Keep your profile professional: Remember that future employers or colleagues may see your public profiles.
*Think before posting anything: Take a moment to consider the impact of your words or images before hitting send.
*Use privacy settings properly: Regularly review and update your account settings to control who sees your content.
*Share useful and positive content: Contribute constructively to your digital communities.

##The Don'ts
 *Do not share personal information: Keep sensitive data like your address, phone number, or financial details private.
 *Do not post offensive or abusive content: Hate speech, bullying, or derogatory remarks have no place online.
 *Do not spread fake news: Verify the source and accuracy of information before sharing it with others.
 *Do not misuse others' photos or data: Always ask for permission before posting pictures of other people or using their work.
 *Do not engage in unnecessary arguments: Avoid getting pulled into toxic comment threads or online fights.
