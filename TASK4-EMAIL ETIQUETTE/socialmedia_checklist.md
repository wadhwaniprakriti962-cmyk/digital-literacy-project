# Social Media Usage Checklist

## ✅ Do’s
- Use strong and secure passwords
- Keep your profile information updated and professional
- Think before posting any content
- Respect others and communicate politely
- Verify information before sharing
- Use privacy settings to control your data

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation
- Avoid using weak passwords
- Do not engage in online arguments or bullying

## Conclusion
Using social media responsibly helps maintain a positive digital presence and ensures online safety.
